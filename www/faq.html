FAQ

What is ANIMEZB?

Animezb is an open source usenet indexer for anime & Japanese media related newsgroups.

Why?

Animezb is meant to complement Fanzub's service. As Fanzub is currently very slow at indexing new uploads and it make sometimes take hours for a new upload to be indexed.

Open Source?

The various parts that make up Animezb are available on Github — http://github.com/animezb.

What newsgroups are indexed?

Anime
alt.binaries.anime
alt.binaries.multimedia.anime
alt.binaries.multimedia.anime.highspeed
As time goes on more categories may be added and indexed.

How often is the index updated?

As most files are posted minutes after they are uploaded to torrent trackers, Animezb makes an effort to keep an up to date index. Once an article is posted to a newsgroup, it should be available for searching, rss and downloading less than a minute after it is posted.

Advanced Search Queries

Animezb currently uses ElasticSearch for searching. The syntax for queries is available here. The available fields for searching are poster, subject, and filename.

How do I download the files?

Animezb does not actually host any files. For a complete guide on downloading from usenet, check out Fanzub's Usenet Guide.
